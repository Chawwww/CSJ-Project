Why SDG 15 Matters

Forests cover 31% of the Earth's land area and are home to 80% of all terrestrial species. They play a crucial role in combating climate change by absorbing carbon dioxide. Yet, deforestation and land degradation continue to threaten these vital ecosystems.

By 2030, we need to protect and restore ecosystems, combat desertification, and halt biodiversity loss to ensure a sustainable future for all life on Earth.
